What is the 50-30-20 rule?

The 50-30-20 rule is a popular financing rule that divides your expenses in three separate categories. Needs, Wants, and Savings, respectively. An in-depth article can be found below.

How to use?

Before you start using this program, you need to know a quick and basic summary of what some terms mean.

  • Budget: A financial estiamte of money, including income and expenditure.
  • Income: The money you receive via work, allowance, etc.
  • Expenses: The amount of money you spend.

This calculator is mainly intended for monthly budgeting.
Once you know your monthly income and expendeture, you may begin to use the calculator to find out what percentage of your money you should be saving and spending on.
